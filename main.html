Shock Wave

Disrupt
Skill: Releases a shock wave that pushes away and damages nearby hostiles while briefly blocking projectiles.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.

Rewind

Disrupt
Glitch: Teleport to the location from 6 seconds prior. Movement speed will be reset.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.

Hacker

Disrupt
Ice Pick: Disrupts nearby enemy minimaps and prevents them from using gadgets.
Passive: Immune to Hacker class. Movement speed increases after activation.

Trickster

Disrupt
Psychosis: Deploy two holographic decoys and get a major speed boost to confuse enemies.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.

Misdirection

Disrupt
Misdirection Device: Emits special sound waves that hide the user’s sound and generate fake noise sources for enemies in range.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.

Trap Master

Disrupt
Electric Trip Wire: Places a high-voltage line that slows enemies and damages anyone standing in the trap.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.

Smoke Bomber

Disrupt
Cluster Smoke Grenade: Creates a smoke field in a target area that can mark enemies.
Passive: After activating skill, movement speed is increased.
