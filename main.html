Maps

Isolated — Points of Interest

Classic CODM BR island; mixed elevation and biomes; frequent chip terminals and vehicle spawns.

Major POIs

Caster Beats

  • Hot drops: Launch Base, Nuclear Plant, Sanitarium
  • Rotate lanes: Pipeline → Docks; Sakura ridge; Countdown plateau
  • Chip timing: watch for mid-zone upgrades leading to power spikes

Blackout — Points of Interest

BO4’s stitched sandbox adapted for mobile; long sightlines and vertical anchors (Construction, Estates).

Major POIs

Caster Beats

  • Power positions: Construction Site, Fracking Tower, Array ridges
  • Island split: Nuketown Island crosses are risky mid-game; watch bridge camps
  • Vehicle value: High—rotate between east/west river splits
